[A Balanced & Complete Life][1]

Yeah, another beautiful Saturday.

I am lucky to meet another thoughtful man who is 55 year old. He shares a story of his camping last couple of days. Through this experience he tells he feels complete and fulfilled either body nor soul and spirit.

He is concerned that nowadays most of people are imbalanced and losing a complete life. Because he thought we human is consisted by three parts, and most of us only completed by 1/3 or 2/3.

  • physical body
  • soul
  • spirit

In the aspect of physical, he says we lack skill of life, man doesn’t fish, hunting, fix roof, cut firewood. Woman doesn’t cook, hunt, plant a garden, raise flowers. We don’t do things physically, but only spend time for gaming, scrolling screen. So we cannot do things by ourselves, we are not completely physically.

Speaking of soul, he suggests to read some poems, listen some classic music, checkout some paintings. Life is more than work, beer, women. There is a beauty out there that filled our soul, if we don’t touch the beauty out there, we are drown, we cannot see the beauty of nature, we cannot feel happy.

If we don’t understand there are something bigger, greater, wiser than we are, then there is a whole chunk of our soul that will never be complete. We need thankfulness, humility in our life. And that’s is spirit.

I cannot agree more. In city, there are only three things: work, eat and sleep. There is no chance i can touch the dirty of land, smell the fresh air in the country, grow flower, enjoy each sunrise and sunset, i feel i am so unnatural in physical and soul. I think the more time i live in this environment the less happy i can feel.

City life just ruins our life, i can feel how cold blood the city people are. They are losing the three parts. Only one thing they care about and that’s money, they don’t talk even live in the same building, there is no help of each other and in fact they don’t need too nowadays.

Yes, how to be completed, it is a problem, for me, for every body!
